Corned Beef Sanwich

Discription

This is an amazing type of sandwich, filled with corned beef, tomato, cheese, and lovely buttered bread. It is easy to make and the ingredients are relatively cheap.

Ingredients

  • Corned Beef
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Place 2 slices of bread side by side
  2. Butter one slice of bread
  3. Place slice of cheese on buttered bread
  4. Cut slice of corned beef to fit on bread and place on top of cheese
  5. Slice tomato so 3 pieces can fit on top of corned beef
  6. place second slice of bread on top of tomato
  7. Enjoy!
