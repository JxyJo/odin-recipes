Homemade Pizza

Description

The pizza is one of the most popular and famous foods in history. There are many variants of the pizza, however, the pepperoni, meatlovers, and hawaiian variants are far superior than any others.The one pictured in this image is a pepperoni pizza. It contains pepperoni, herbs, mozzarella, tomato paste, and pizza base.

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • Herbs
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato Paste
  • Pizza Base

Steps

  1. Smear tomato paste all over the pizza base
  2. Sprinkle stringy cheese on top of the tomato paste
  3. Add pepperoni in between the cheese
  4. Bake the pizza in an oven for about 10 minutes
  5. Sprinkle the herbs on top of the baked pizza
  6. Enjoy!
